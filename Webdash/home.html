From Raw to Fruitful: Preprocessing the Data

Our analysis includes Stores Info, optimized to 413 records for streamlined performance. The Per Day Sales metric highlights the economy of overall QSR operations on a timescale, while Store-Wise Sales performance, strategically extracted and optimized to 6,495 records from the original 632,198, achieves over 90% optimization. We also provide Location-Based Sales Information to identify opportunities geographically and evaluate Promotional Activities to assess their impact on overall sales.
